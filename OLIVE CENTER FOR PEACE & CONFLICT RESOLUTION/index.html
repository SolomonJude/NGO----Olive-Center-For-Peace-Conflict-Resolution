Who we are

Olive Center for Peace and Conflict Resolution is duly registered with Corporate Affairs Commission, RC. No.185055. We are a legal entity in Nigeria

Organize seminars/workshops to show importance of leadership to unity and peace.

Expose and exploit mechanisms of conflict resolution in order to unite and integrate our nation.

Carry out development projects in areas of health, water, economic empowerment and infrastructure because of the strong correlation between development and sustainable peace.

Motivate good leadership in Nigeria and Africa by rewarding exemplary selfless leaders who encourage a peaceful society.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does Olive Center for Peace and Conflict Resolution do?

We are a non-governmental organization involved in educating and sensitizing Nigerians on the priceless need for peace and unity and as well educate children on human rights.

Does Olive Center for Peace and Conflict Resolution provide grant?

We don't provide grant or assist with fundraising except we are funded to empower the less privileged like widows, youths, orphans and vulnerable children.

What year was Olive Center for Peace and Conflict Resolution founded?

We were founded in the year 2000 but incorporated into CAC as a trustee in 2022.

Is Olive Center for Peace and Conflict Resolution a registered company?

Yes, we are registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission with RC. NO.185055

What is the maximum amount i can donate?

There is no fixed amount to how much you would love to donate.

What are we for?

We are for a better Nigeria.
